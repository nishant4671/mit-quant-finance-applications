# 1. Time Has Different Prices

Suppose you can invest money for different lengths of time.

| Maturity | Market Interest Rate |
|-----------|---------------------:|
| 1 Year | 4.0% |
| 2 Years | 4.5% |
| 3 Years | 5.0% |
| 5 Years | 5.8% |
| 10 Years | 6.2% |

Question:

Do investors demand the same return for every maturity?

No.

The market usually requires different returns for different investment horizons.

This collection of interest rates forms the yield curve.
